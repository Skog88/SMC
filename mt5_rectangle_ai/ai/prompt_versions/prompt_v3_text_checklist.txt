=== CHECKLIST ===
Answer YES or NO to each. Base answers strictly on the data above.

1. clean_sweep
   Did the candle marked [SWEEP] cleanly wick through the swept level (Δlow < 0 for long,
   Δhigh > 0 for short) and close back inside (Δclose > 0 for long, Δclose < 0 for short)?
   Consider: wick size vs body size — a wick at least 1.5× the body is cleaner.

2. liquidity_pool
   Based on the mechanical context (swept_level_type), is this a pool of liquidity
   (double/triple top/bottom or session level) rather than a lone single swing?
   YES = swept_level_type is double_*, triple_*, session_high, or session_low.
   NO  = single_swing only.

3. fvg_present
   Is there evidence of a Fair Value Gap (imbalance) in the candles shown?
   Look for: a candle where the high of candle[i-2] is below the low of candle[i] (bullish FVG)
   or the low of candle[i-2] is above the high of candle[i] (bearish FVG), within the entry zone.
   Also confirmed by ob_fvg_overlap in context.

4. ob_visible
   Is the candle marked [OB] clearly the last opposing candle before an impulsive move?
   YES if: the OB candle is followed by a strong directional run of 3+ candles covering
   significant distance toward the swept level.
   NO if: the move to the level was gradual, choppy, or the OB candle is surrounded by
   overlapping candles on both sides.

5. htf_confirmed
   Is the htf_bias in the context aligned with the trade direction?
   YES = (direction=long AND htf_bias=bullish) OR (direction=short AND htf_bias=bearish).
   NO  = htf_bias=neutral OR opposing direction.

6. draw_visible
   Based on the candle sequence, is there evidence of unswept structure in the trade direction?
   For a long: are there visible swing highs above the current price that price has not yet reached?
   For a short: are there visible swing lows below the current price?
   Use the Δhigh/Δlow values to identify unmitigated extremes.

7. would_trade
   Considering all 6 answers above and the mechanical context, would a disciplined SMC trader
   take this setup? This is your holistic sanity check.
   NO if: the sweep was weak (tiny wick), the OB was mitigated (ob_mitigation_count > 1),
   the zone is wrong (zone_type conflicts with direction), or the structure is unconvincing.
